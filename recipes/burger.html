description

This classic burger recipe features juicy beef patties, melted cheese, and fresh toppings, all stacked in a toasted bun. It's quick, satisfying, and perfect for any meal.

ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Burger buns
  • Cheese slices
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Season ground beef with salt and pepper; form patties.
  2. Cook patties on a grill or pan until desired doneness.
  3. Add cheese slices near the end to melt.
  4. Toast burger buns lightly.
  5. Assemble with lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and sauces.
  6. Serve immediately.
