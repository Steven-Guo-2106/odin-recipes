Description

Lasagna is a comforting, hearty dish that layers pasta, savory sauce, cheese, and other flavorful ingredients to create a satisfying meal. Traditionally made with wide, flat noodles, this dish is filled with alternating layers of rich meat sauce (often made from ground beef or pork), a creamy ricotta or béchamel sauce, and melted mozzarella. It’s seasoned with a blend of herbs like basil, oregano, and garlic, which infuse the dish with deep, aromatic flavors. Baked until golden and bubbling, lasagna is the perfect dish for family dinners, gatherings, or special occasions. Whether you prefer a classic meat version or a vegetarian alternative, this dish offers a delicious balance of textures and tastes that are sure to please any palate.

Ingredients

Meat sauce

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 small onion
  • 2-3 cloves of garlic
  • 24oz tomato sauce
  • 14oz(1 can) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  • 1 tsp basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Cheese Mixture

  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tbsp chopped parsley

Lasagna Base

  • 12 lasagna sheets
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • fresh basil and parsley

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook ground beef with onion and garlic; add tomato sauce.
  3. Boil lasagna noodles until al dente.
  4. Mix ricotta with egg, salt, and pepper.
  5. Layer sauce, noodles, ricotta, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
  6. Repeat layers and finish with mozzarella on top.
  7. Bake for 30–40 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  8. Rest 10 minutes before serving.
