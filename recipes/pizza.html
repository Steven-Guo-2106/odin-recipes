description

This homemade pizza recipe features a crispy crust, rich tomato sauce, gooey mozzarella cheese, and your favorite toppings. It's easy to make and perfect for a fun, delicious meal at home.

ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • tomato sauce
  • mozzarella cheese
  • olive oil
  • toppings of your choice

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough.
  3. Spread tomato sauce over the dough.
  4. Add mozzarella and your favorite toppings.
  5. Bake for 10 to 15 minutes until crust is golden.
  6. Slice and serve hot.
